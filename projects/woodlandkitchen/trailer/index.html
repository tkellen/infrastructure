The Woodland Kitchen

From-scratch recipes & workshops from my rustic forest cottage.

Hi, I'm Tara! I'm a cookbook author and homesteader.

I live on a small plot of land in the Green Mountains of Vermont in a timber frame cottage I built by hand.

About a year ago I set out to explore the land around me through my favorite medium: food.

I wanted to cook over a fire, forage for wild edible plants, and create recipes inspired by the seasons of New England.

I've spent the last twelve months exploring this idea, falling in love with the flavors and foodways of this place I call home.

Starting this fall, I invite you to join me at thewoodlandkitchen.com to share in these culinary adventures.

By signing up below, you'll be the first to know when the blog officially launches and you'll receive a welcome letter that includes one of my favorite recipes.

It is my hope that the Woodland Kitchen inspires you as it does me, making your life a little more beautiful, a little more delicious, and a lot more wild.

Let's be friends!
